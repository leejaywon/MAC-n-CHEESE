# Track 2 — ICML-Style Review

This is the frozen output schema, not an unfilled submission result.
`run_review.py` resolves every identity, comment, score, and trace field from the
reviewed paper and evidence; generated reviews must contain no placeholder text.

## Paper and Evidence Identity

- Review Agent name/version:
- `review-agent.md` path/hash:
- Original paper identity/media type/hash/bytes/page count:
- Derived Markdown identity/hash/converter:
- Evidence bundle reviewed:
- External citation snapshot digest:
- Frozen review identity/verdict digest:

Record concrete values generated by S6, including both original PDF and derived
Markdown identities. Local paths are provenance only and are excluded from the
content-addressed review identity.

## Summary

Summarize the paper's stated contribution and empirical/theoretical scope, then
state the decisive evidence-grounded review outcome.

## Strengths

Include only strengths grounded in supported claims or explicit paper spans.

## Weaknesses

Include proven findings and grounded scope/positioning limitations. Convert
unproven concerns into author questions. In `best` mode, cover problem–method
fit, claim–experiment alignment, experimental validity, scope/generalization,
and design-choice/ablation justification.

## Questions for the Authors

Provide at most five numbered, paper-specific questions. State what evidence or
clarification would change the assessment.

## Scores

- Soundness:
- Presentation:
- Significance:
- Originality:
- Overall recommendation:
- Confidence:

Use the platform scales directly: four dimensions 1–4, Overall 1–6, and
Confidence 1–5. Give one evidence-backed rationale per score.

## Ethics and Limitations

Report injection handling separately from scientific quality, and state limits
of the available evidence.

## Evidence Trace

Resolve every cited claim/finding identifier, record S1–S6 execution, source and
evidence hashes, sanitation traces, citation snapshot digest, score grounds, and
verdict-label digest. When a scientific committee succeeds, also record its
role-level prompt/response provenance and aggregate judgment identity; when it
falls back, record only a concise non-secret fallback reason.

Map each central claim to a paper section, table, figure, or saved result. Flag anything that cannot be verified.
